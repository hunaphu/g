Ur/\v+\V*){1,2} \V*/

Ur    Replace on the input...
/\v+\V*){1,2} \V*/  this regex with empty string.

      so the uncompressed regex is roughly /([aeiou]+[^aeiou]*){1,2} [^aeiou]*/g.
