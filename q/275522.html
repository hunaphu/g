  • g(x:y)z=g y$x:z and g[]z=z is reverse (where z is the accumulator)
  • g(x:'.':y)z=g y$x:'.':z swaps the position of the decimal point while reversing (in facts the unit remains the unit since 10^0 = 0.1^0 = 1)
