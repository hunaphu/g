A monadic Link that accepts the disks as an unordered list of a prefix of the natural numbers and yields a list of instructions. Each instruction is:

[[FromPeg, ToPeg], DiskCount]

where the initially full peg is 0 while 1 and 2 are the others (although we only use 0 and 1 anyway :p)

Try it online!

How?

Pick up all the disks from the "Source Peg" and put them on another peg, the "Buffer Peg", then repeatedly (a) pick up as many disks as needed from the Buffer Peg to reach the largest disk and place them onto the Source Peg, and (b) pick up one less disk than in (a) from the Source Peg and place them on the Buffer Peg. When executing the last iteration part (b) is a no-op so we don't output it.

This results in many redundant moves for any initially sorted run :p
