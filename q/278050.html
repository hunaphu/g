Regex parts

None of the following expressions should give a match on the string or its reverse counterpart:

  1. (F|X)(.*\1){5} → more than 5 F or 5 X
  2. (O.*){7} → more than 6 O
  3. ^(....)*.?F(O|....O....)X → horizontal or diagonal FOX
  4. ^(....)*.?..F..O..X → anti-diagonal FOX
  5. F...O...X → vertical FOX

Note that expressions 3 and 4 are actually factorized as:

^(....)*.?(F(O|....O....)|..F..O..)X
