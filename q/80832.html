f=N[∫1/x!{x,-1,∞},#+1]&

Explanation

N[               , # + 1] 
  ∫1/x!{x,-1,∞}

First step takes Numeric of the rest, with # (first parameter) + 1 precision. ! (factorial) does what you'd expect. {x, -1, Infinity} sets the bounds for the (strangely formatted) Integral.
