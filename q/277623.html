A function taking an argument in the form [12,00] and returning a newline separated string.

It forms a canvas of 20 lines of 39 spaces plus newline. . It then draws from the tip of the minute hand at iteration -3 to the centre 0 and then on to tip of the hour hand 2 by replacing characters.

The centre of the clock is at index 420 (I didn't plan it that way, it just happened!) There is an array of 6 magic numbers for the offsets corresponding to half a revolution of the hand. the other half revolution is managed by negating these offsets by multiplying by -1 raised to the power h+g/6 (due to Ruby priority rules, -1 is represented by ~0.)
