L$`\b((\w)+)\b(?=(.)*(?<=(?(2)$)((?<-2>\2)|.)*\1(?(3)^)(?<-3>(?<-2>\2)|.)*))

Find all of the words that are subsequences of the remaining string. .NET balancing groups are used twice, firstly ?<-2> matches the subsequence and secondly ?<-3> ensures that the original word is skipped at the right place when matching the subsequence. (Note that the lookbehind is processed right-to-left, so that the (?(2)$) is actually the last part of the regex.) Each result consists of the word prefixed to the original input.
