    s[R](            //     replacing in s:
      RegExp(        //       the (only) occurrence of w
        `\\b${w}\\b` //       surrounded by word boundaries

      w[R](          //       with w where ...

Find all of the words that are subsequences of the remaining string. .NET balancing groups are used twice, firstly ?<-2> matches the subsequence and secondly ?<-3> ensures that the original word is skipped at the right place when matching the subsequence.
